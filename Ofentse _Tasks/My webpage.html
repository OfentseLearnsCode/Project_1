Optimism is key

Let's define optimism.

Optimism refers to a hopeful and confident outlook on success and a bright future are characteristics of optimism. Optimists often see difficulties as temporary setbacks or opportunities for growth.
Even the worst day holds the promise that "tomorrow will probably be better.

Signs of optimism

Optimism can be shown in a number of ways. The following is what optimists commonly do:

  • Being active at work.
  • Look at the bright side
  • Take criticism well
  • View challenge as opportunities

Ways to be optimistic

  • Be mindful of your emotions
  • Practice gratitude
  • Find other optimists
